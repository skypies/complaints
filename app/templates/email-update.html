You're getting this email because this email address has been registered on the site http://stop.jetnoise.net (was complaints.serfr1.org).

A few astute readers noticed some discrepencies with the by-town totals yesterday; as a resut, I found a bunch of profiles that had 'old' address data. I fixed them up, and there are are 101 fewer inhabitants of town 'Unknown'. So I've regenerated the stats I sent out yesterday, and attached them to this email.

This should not have affected the data sent into SFO; but I'm working to confirm that. They get the raw string, whether in old or new format, which for almost everyone contained a full address. But my by-town summary only understands the new format.

A huge amount of thanks for all the people who volunteered their window ledges to house ADS-B gizmos ! If I've not been back in touch with you yet, I will; I'm trying to figure out what a nice, regular distribution would look like on the map.

Not surprisingly though, almost everyone who offered just so happens to live directly under SERFR. So the distribution looks more like a single straight line from EPICK to MENLO, rather than a grid, and won't work out too well for triangulation.

So if you happen to live, say, up on Skyline, or in San Francisco, or South San Francisco, or Oakland, or San Mateo, or San Jose - and you're willing to host a gizmo - I'd love to hear from you :)

 - Adam
